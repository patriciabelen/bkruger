Visualizing

Barbara

Kruger

This study explores the relationship between data and visual analysis. Can visuals be considered data? This project begins to investigate conceptual connections between Digital Humanities and visual imagery.

Artist Barbara Kruger is widely known for combining photographic imagery and text to critique culture and challenge the viewer. Using a corpus of thirty of her works from the 1980's, I've analyzed the text (word frequency and grammatical structure) and visual qualities (images, subjects, themes, and sentiments), resulting in this set of data visualizations. Perhaps, viewing Kruger's work in this manner will lead to new revelations about her work.
